
   YAEC 1.5a - startup/startup.txt
   -------------------------------

   This directory should contain :

   yaec.i          - asm includes.
   startup.o       - startup code.
   library.o       - lib-startup code.
   device.o        - dev-startup code.
   generate.script - handles assembling/linking

   For normal operation, you dont need to worry about theese.
   Its all automatic.
