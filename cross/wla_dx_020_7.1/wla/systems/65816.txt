WLA 65816 knows the following 65816 mnemonics. If you think something's missing
from the list or something is totally wrong, please send me (vhelin@cc.hut.fi)
mail!


NOTES:

- You can't use '[' and ']' instead of '(' and ')' as these are different
  addressing modes in 65816.
- Use REP/SEP to affect the following "XYZ #x" mnemonics' value sizes.
- Use .8BIT, .16BIT and .24BIT to indicate the computation size when
  WLA fails.

x = 8bit value.
? = 16bit value.
& = 24bit value.
