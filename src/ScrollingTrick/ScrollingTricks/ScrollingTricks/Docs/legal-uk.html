  • If the full version(!) of the game is free (Freeware), but the archive is larger than 10 MByte and/or I don't have any possibility to download it from Internet, then what said at point one applies. That is, you either have to send me a free copy of the game or 5 DEM (5000 LIT, 3 US$) in cash.
