    Anouncing Power Programs Amiga Features:

      To help keep the Amiga community together, Power Programs will start Amiga Features. These pages will contain tips,tricks and articles on all thing Amiga related. Be it a way to do a specific trick in C, beginners advice for AREXX or even a small rant regarding all those takeovers, Amiga Features is your place. You can expect to see articles here soon!

      If you want to submit an article for Amiga Features, please send an outline of the article, and a matching category. If the article is fit to be placed, we will request you to send the full article. Submit all articles to jeroen@infothuis.nl. If no existing category matches your article, feel free to name a category yourself.
