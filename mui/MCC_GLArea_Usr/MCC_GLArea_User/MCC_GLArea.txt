Compiled for 68040/68060
          or 68030 with a 68881/2

Introduction:
-------------
This class is a sub-class of Area and let you display OpenGL (StormMesa)
renderings in an MUI environnement.

Requirement:
------------
You need:
-agl.library,aglu.library,aglut.library (StormMesa libraries)
-68040/68060 or 68030 with 68881/2
-MUI 3.x
-Kickstart 2.04+
-Some Fast RAM

Recommended:
-A CybergraphX card
-A Warp3D Card (not tested)

Installation:
-------------
Copy GLArea.mcc (or another cpu version) to MUI:Libs/mui
There is no Global preference for GLArea.mcc (I don't know how to write a mcp...)
