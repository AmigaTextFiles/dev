Requirements

AutodocViewer Lite v2.0 requires atleast AmigaOS v3.5 and MC68020 CPU to start. The memory requirements and free disk space depends on how AutodocViewer Lite is customized.

AutodocViewer also requires autodocs formatted with Autodoc Style Guide by Commodore Electronics Ltd. and it's recommended that the user also use assembly includes or c includes. The AmigaOS 3.5 autodocs and includes are found on AmigaDeveloper CD v2.1, other developer archives like MUI, RTG etc. are found on Aminet.

It's recommended that the users computer has atleast an 68040/40 CPU, 4Mb of free memory and about 2Mb of free disk space (includes and Autodocs are not included in the recommended free disk space).
