The Smart Display Window

Now have a look to the Smart Display Window.

The Smart Display Window is the fastest way to see how your messages will look once the catalog will be made.
It shows you your message, including all the special chars, like the "\n" or the %ld.
NOTE: You have also another way to show your message live, click the Show gadget near the message you are writing from the Main Window.
