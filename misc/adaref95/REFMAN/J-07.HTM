J.7 At Clauses -- TOC

Syntax
(1)
       at_clause ::= for direct_name use at expression;
Static Semantics
(2)
An at_clause of the form ``for x use at y;'' is equivalent to an attribute_definition_clause of the form ``for x'Address use y;''.

Subclauses
