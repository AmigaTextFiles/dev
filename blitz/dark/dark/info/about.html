About

Finally i managed to get back to this project, which actually has been frozen for a loooong while. Motivated by the great iBrowse and all other new amiga browsers, i want to demonstrate, that a basic based language like blitz has the possibilities to write such range of software. This is a very early demo release for some good friends..

please try it out and let me know, what you think.
This software is NOT complete !

technical, bugs & co.

  • sometimes spaces appear or disappear (uh, strange)
  • url resolving is sometimes not correct.
  • the blitz-tag is still not supported. Soon ...
  • make sure, that you've got 'ed' and 'more' in your c: drawer
