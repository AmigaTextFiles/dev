Interface

AppBuilder uses a simple interface that allow you to have all under your control and into a single place, what you see in the main interface is what you have at your fingertips to build your applications.


 The above image shows the application main window, as you can see the interface is splitted into sections.

In the left side we have 3 groups : the top group is used to manage your project, the middle one is used to set/unset compile switches (saved with your project) and the bottom one holds the configuration button we have seen earlier and some other button not related with the project.

The right side is used to setup your project, to select for which platform(s) you want to build your app and what plugin(s) you want to include in your distribution package.

The "App Resources" and “External Data” groups allow you to include files & folders to your distribution package, but we will talk about them later.
