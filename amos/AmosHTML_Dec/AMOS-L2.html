It was on the night of 04-Dec-97, when no creatures were stirring, and all was
silent. Suddenly, Jens jumped out from behind a rock and wouldnt stop shouting
about "Foundation.." until I was forced to knock them out:
-> Ok, now I think it's time for a little help to foundation, that bloddy
-> 'mush' has made me aware of :)

-> 1) What's a 'peasant-hut' for (what do you get from it) ??..

Well, if you want your population to expand and grow, not to mention
position itself around the island better, you need to build these for your
people to live in. You have to note, the master house can only hold x
number of people before it starts to become damaged to mass overcrowding.

-> 2) When I get to about year 23/24 I begin to loose people quite fast
-> (mission 2), I've build a hospital, and have plenty of food in stock ??

You also have to keep their morality high, if they have no task, or they
get sick of eating wheat over and over again, then their morale will be so
low, they might decide to kill themselves!

Go into GAME STATISTICS and click on the one with the cross, as it shows
you every person who has died, and what they died off.

Are you sure your population didnt get a virus of some kind? The guys have
flies buzzing round their heads if they do.

Also, if the enemy attacked you, because you had no guards and such, they
just slaughtered you, hehe!

-> 3) Is it possible to stop a fire in a building before the building gets
-> destroyed ??

Yes, simply summon a wizard to the building to put it out.

-> 4) I can't increse the population (also se point 2), what am I doing wrong
?

See above. You have to expand.
