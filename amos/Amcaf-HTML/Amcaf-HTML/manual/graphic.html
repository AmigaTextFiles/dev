Graphic Commands and Functions


AMCAF contains a lot of graphic commands, many of which you surely can use in your games or demos. However, you should be aware that most commands do not check the parameters you use!
This improves the speed of some commands. Remember to save your commands, due to the fact that a small mistake can crash your computer.
